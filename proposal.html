Independent Project Proposal

Project Name:

Bank of America App Redesign

Overview:

The focus of this project is to redesign both aesthetics and interaction on the Bank of America App for the iPhone. This exercise will focus on the process of user research and iteration while challenging my ability to create pleasing aesthetics.
Lo-fi and Hi-fi Prototype: Sketch

Audience:

Users who use the Bank of America mobile iPhone app
26.43 millions of mobile banking users of BoA in 2018.
69.3% of Millennials in US use Mobile Banking.
47.9% of Gen X in US use Mobile Banking.

Timeline (with Deliverables):

  1. Week of Feb. 9th: User Research
  2. Week of Feb. 16th: Continuation of User Research and Ideation Stage
  3. Week of Feb. 23rd: Lo-fi Prototype and Feedback
  4. Week of Mar. 1st: Hi-fi Prototype and Feedback
